## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer shopping behavior across shopping malls using Power BI.
The analysis focuses on gender, age group, product categories, and payment methods.

## Objectives
- Analyze shopping distribution by gender and age
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Study payment method preferences
- Derive actionable business insights

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Visualization

## Dashboard Pages
### Page 1: Customer Overview
- Gender-wise shopping distribution
- Age group analysis
- Revenue and quantity insights

### Page 2: Category & Payment Analysis
- Category-wise revenue and quantity
- Payment method distribution
- Payment behavior by gender and age group

## Dataset Information
The dataset used in this project is **not publicly shared** due to data privacy and credential restrictions.

## Key Insights
- Female customers contribute higher revenue
- Age group 55+ shows highest purchasing power
- Clothing is the top revenue-generating category
- Cash is the most preferred payment method
